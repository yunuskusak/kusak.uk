Featured Open-Source Infrastructure

kusak-infra — Deterministic HFT Execution Engine

At the core of our trading infrastructure lies KUSAK Lock-Free SPSC HFT Infrastructure Repository — a production‑grade, open‑source C++17 engine built for Binance WebSocket integration, nanosecond‑precision hardware timing, and lock‑free concurrency. Every component is designed to operate at the hardware limit with zero heap allocation in the critical path.

The repository serves as both a reference implementation for institutional trading desks and a foundation for our broader algorithmic trading bot and low‑latency trading research. Developers and quantitative teams are encouraged to explore the source code, documentation, and benchmarks available in the public repository.

Architecture

KUSAK Ltd.'s technical foundation is built upon the proprietary system architecture designed by our founder, Yunus Kuşak. His expertise in C++17 low‑latency systems, lock‑free concurrency models, and hardware‑sensing software design forms the backbone of every product we ship.

From the nanosecond‑precision trading engine to the real‑time OSINT pipelines, the architectural decisions are guided by a single principle: predictability at scale. We do not rely on bloated frameworks, garbage collectors, or third‑party black boxes. Instead, we build from first principles — a philosophy that enables us to iterate faster, debug deeper, and scale further than our competitors.

This approach is not limited to a single project. kusak-infra is the public flagship of a much broader infrastructure vision — spanning quantitative finance, defence‑grade intelligence tooling, and distributed systems research — all architected to meet the demands of institutional clients and real‑world markets.
