Project YYY

Signal defense architecture.

Project YYY is a deterministic signal defense engine positioned between external telemetry sources and internal operational systems.

Instead of collecting information indiscriminately, the platform validates, sanitizes, classifies, and routes incoming signals through controlled execution paths.

Every external signal is treated as untrusted until verification is complete. Collection is secondary. Operational integrity is primary.
